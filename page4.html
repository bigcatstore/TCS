The '74 Shirt

A Classic design comes back to life

Back in 1974 team merchandise was a new concept and the Tyrrell team were one of the first  to start selling items for fans at race circuits. The very first Tyrrell T-Shirt design to go on sale was the Tyrrell 007 design. This shirt was printed back and front in three colours and if you scour the internet hard enough you will find photos of both Jackie Stewart and Patrick Depailler wearing this design! 

In addition to our original race car artwork products we wanted to bring you some team related items and so with the kind permission of Bob Tyrrell we have re-mastered the artwork for this design and produced a limted print run of this iconic shirt.

These shirts are officially licensed by Tyrrell Promotions Ltd and it's great to have this retro item back on sale.

So treat yourself to a small piece of 1970's raceday cool with the classic '74 shirt. This item can be worn with flares for the full '70s effect....
